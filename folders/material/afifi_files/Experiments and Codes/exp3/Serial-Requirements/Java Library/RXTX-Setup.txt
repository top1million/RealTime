according to your platform type (64 or 32) pick the right folder within TXRX:

- Copy "rxtxSerial.dll" into "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.7.0_80\jre\bin"
- Copy "RXTXComm.jar" into "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.7.0_80\jre\lib\ext"
- Copy "RXTXComm.jar" into "lib" folder within your project directory

*Ignore the warning about driver version mismatch at the run-time
